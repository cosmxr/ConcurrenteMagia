Welcome to Concurrente Magia

About the Game

Concurrente Magia is a magical adventure game where you face various challengers in different levels. Each level increases in difficulty, and you must use your spells wisely to defeat your opponents.

Spells

There are three types of spells you can use:

  • Attack Spells: These spells deal damage to your opponent.
  • Defense Spells: These spells increase your defense percentage and can boost your attack.
  • Healing Spells: These spells restore your health.

Magical Event Levels

As you progress through the game, you will encounter different challengers:

  • Level 1: Duel with a wizard. Challenger Health: 100, Attack Points: 10, Defense Percentage: 10.
  • Level 2: Battle with a troll. Challenger Health: 200, Attack Points: 20, Defense Percentage: 20.
  • Level 3: Fight a dragon. Challenger Health: 300, Attack Points: 30, Defense Percentage: 30.
